Submission of Form


A statement form is required when submitting an article to International Journal on Biodiversity Watch.
The statement does not mention that the submitted article is an original work of the author which has never published nor is it in the process of publication elsewhere.
As well, it states that the author(s) agree(s) to transfer the copyright to the publisher.
Those who submit the article are regarded to agree with the contents of the statement form.
The completed form could be scanned, and please send its file to the Editors in .jpg or .PDF format.
The Statement Form can be downloaded from the website of the Journal.
