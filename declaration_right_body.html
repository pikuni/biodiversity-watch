Declaration by the Author


Along with the manuscript, the author should fill and send a statement form mentioning the submitted article is an original work of the author(s) and has never been published or is not in the process of its publication.
As well, the author(s) should state that he/she/they agree(s) to transfer the copyright to the publisher. The filled form could be scanned, and send its file to the Editors in .jpg or .pdf format.
