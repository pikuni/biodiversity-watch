VRM Foundation International is a Policy Research Institute dedicated to holistic management of conservation and promoting community based natural resource management. The Foundation has been registered under Indian Trust Act. VRM Foundation International organizes global summit on biodiversity conservation, initiate environmental education awareness raising campaign, undertakes action research on biodiversity issues brings out international journal on Biodiversity Watch and periodic newspaper on forest issues. The Foundation emphasize that participatory natural resource management approach is key to uphold unique biodiversity resources of India.

Greater efforts are made to facilitate community led programme for environmental restoration, conservation and sustainable livelihood and training and support in sustainable agriculture and forest management agro forestry, energy conservation, sustainable income generation including eco-tourism and rehabilitation of degraded forest. The Foundation initiate partnership with different national and international agencies working on integrated natural resource management. The Foundation has created international track record by successfully organizing First Global Summit on Biodiversity and Sustainable Development, Gloss 2008, 7-9, February 2009 at Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India.
